FAQ

Quick answers. If you’re unsure, book a 10–15 min call.

Federal vs Provincial — which one do I need?

It depends on where you will operate and how you want name protection handled. We’ll ask a few questions and recommend the best option for your situation.

How long does incorporation take?

Typical turnaround is 24–72 hours, depending on jurisdiction processing times, name availability, and how quickly you provide required information.

What information do you need from me?

Usually: director/officer details, registered office information, ownership basics, and your preferred company name (or numbered company).

Do you provide legal or tax advice?

We provide corporate services and filing support. If legal or tax advice is required, we can coordinate with partner professionals.

Can you help if I’m not in Canada?

Many incorporations can be handled remotely. Requirements vary; we’ll confirm during intake.
